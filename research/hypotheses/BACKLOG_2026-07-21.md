# Hypothesis Backlog & Triage — 2026-07-21 (operator idea intake)

The operator submitted two idea batches on 2026-07-21:

- **Batch 1** — 9 "unconventional" ideas, operator-numbered H025–H033
  (information compression, consensus collapse, market memory, energy
  accumulation, decision density, cross-market echo, regime fatigue,
  market surprise, meta self-confidence).
- **Batch 2** — a 60-item categorized catalog, operator-numbered H001–H060.

**Numbering policy (binding).** The registry
(`research/results/registry.json`) is the single source of truth for
hypothesis IDs. Registry IDs H001–H024 were already assigned before these
batches existed (registry H001 = liquidity sweep, FAILED p=0.63 — *not*
EMA200). Operator-catalog numbers are therefore cited below as `c-HXXX`
(batch 2) and `b1-HXXX` (batch 1), and every idea that survives triage gets
a **registry** ID. Two were registered today; the rest hold *reserved* IDs
here until someone commits to run them — a backlog row is NOT a
registration. Promotion from this file to the registry requires a full
hypothesis doc with a decision rule written before any result exists
(CLAUDE.md rule 1).

**Execution policy (binding).** Nothing in this file authorizes running 60
backtests. The house pipeline is: pre-register → build runner → chronological
TRAIN/TEST (H008c standard) + walk-forward → verdict against the
pre-registered rule → commit result (positive or negative). Ideas matching
the dead list are refused by pointing at the measured kill, per CLAUDE.md.

- **DEAD** — duplicate of a measured kill (CLAUDE.md dead list). Refused by pointing at the evidence; a revival requires a NEW pre-registration that is demonstrably distinct.
- **LIVE** — already implemented in the production system; nothing to test beyond what the shadow book / forward demo already measures.
- **REG** — already a registry hypothesis (ID given).
- **RESERVED HXXX** — genuinely new; holds a reserved registry ID below.
- **BLOCKED** — testable in principle but blocked by a standing rule (frozen thresholds, forward-sample integrity, missing data).
- **TOOLING** — diagnostic/instrumentation, not a falsifiable hypothesis.
